## What's included

- **Core:** Basic, Scientific, Date & Time
- **Domain:** Accounting, Civil, Electrical, Physics, Business
- **Visualization:** Graph Plotter, 3D Shapes, Math & Stats
- **Lifestyle:** Health & Fitness, Unit Conversion, Currency Converter

All data stays on your device. No sign-up, no ads, no tracking.
